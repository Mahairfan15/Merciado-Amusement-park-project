CLEANINESS AND HYGIENE

Removal of trash and debris ensures the space is free of potential hazards that could cause injuries, like tripping hazards or broken glass. Cleaning and disinfecting surfaces helps prevent the spread of germs and bacteria that can lead to illnesses, especially among children whose immune systems are still developing.
