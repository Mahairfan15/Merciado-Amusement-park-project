How to Book Your ticket!

- Visit the Booking Page

Click on the "Book Tickets" button in the homepage.

- Choose Your Visit Date

Use the calendar to select the date you plan to visit the park.

- Select Ticket Type

Choose from the available ticket types (Weekday, Weekend, or Family Pack).

- Enter Visitor Details

Fill in your name, email, and phone number in the form.

- Add Number of Tickets

Select the number of adults and children tickets required.

- Make Payment

Select a payment option and complete the process.

- Review and Confirm

Check all the entered details and click book now button.

- Receive Confirmation

You will see a confirmation message.
